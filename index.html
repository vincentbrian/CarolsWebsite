Carol Cares

dog walking and pet sitting service

We are passionate about providing excellent and loving care for your pet. We treat your pet like they are our own. We service Norwalk, Darien, New Canaan, Westport and South Wilton. We pride ourselves on maintaining long-lasting relationships with our animal clients and their owners.

What We Do

Pet Sitting

We provide in-your-home care for your pets where they are most comfortable verses a stressful stay at a kennel. We will feed, administer medication, play, walk, pick up mail, water plants.

Dog Walking

We will leash walk you dog in your neigborhood or take them to a park. Your dog gets individual attention and playtime, fresh water and if instructed a feeding.

Specialty

Don't have time to take your dog to the vet? We can pick up and drop off your pet at groomers, at the vet for appontments, etc.

If you dont have time to take your pet to the vet or groomers, we can provide the transportation.

We apporate 7 days a week

Hi, my name is Carol and I would like to tell you a little about myself. I have always loved animals all my life. Growing up I wasn’t allowed to have a dog or a cat so I would go to a friends house and spend time with my friend and their dog. I got my first dog when I was in my twenties. He was a toy poodle puppy. Was so small that he couldn’t get out of the box like his brothers and sisters. He was very smart and lived to 17.

My second dog was Pomeranian that I rescued and was about six years old. His name was Cinci. The person gave him up because he had liver issues. He was in and out of the vets for the liver for about five years until I had to say goodbye to him.

My third and present dog is another rescue dog. He was taken from a horder who had 10 kinds of big and small dogs and he would have to fight for food. He is still comes to work with me everyday.

I also had a dog that even though she belonged to the person that I worked for back Then I have always loved her like she was one of my own. She was a Bichon and her name was Snuggles. AKA the Snugs.

It has now been 16yrs. Since I started my dog walking business. I love working with animals and hope to continue doing so for as long as I can.
